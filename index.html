Shadows of Aton

Become a pirate in the midst of a crumbling empire.

The Empire of Aton has ruled for many a glorious year. Its culture, riches, and splendor were unmatched throughout the lands. However, civil strife and disaster threaten to undermine its stability. All while the world begins to enter a new age.

Build a fleet to rule the seas in turn-based ship combat.

Will you pilot a fast, maneuverable sloop, outrunning the Imperial gunships? Or will you hide behind a multi-deck fortress, outgunning all those who enter your range?

Take a side among those who wish to profit at the empire's expense.

Empires don't rise and fall in a vacuum. Will you aid a foreign power in their attempts to claim the Empire's territory, or will fight to retain tarnished Imperial glory? Or will you search for and find another path?
